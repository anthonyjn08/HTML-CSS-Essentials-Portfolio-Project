Kobe Bryant: #8 vs #24

It is well known that during his 20 stay at the LA Lakers Bryant wore two jersey numbers. For seasons 1996-97 until 2005-06 Bryant wore number 8. From 2006-07 until his retirement in 2016, Bryant wore the number 24. Fans, players and coaches alike will debate which version of Kobe was their favourite, or which version was better, but here are the numbers.
